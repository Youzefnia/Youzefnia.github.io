About

I'm a Medical Writer and Research Associate with a background in medical biotechnology (MSc, Tehran University of Medical Sciences) and laboratory sciences (BSc, Kerman University of Medical Sciences). Most of my work has been about translation in the broadest sense — turning dense, technical science into writing that's accurate and genuinely easy to read, from research proposals to health-focused content strategy.

Along the way, AI tools became part of how I research, write, and think — prompt engineering, content automation, working with LLMs daily. That habit of describing a problem clearly and iterating toward a good answer is exactly what pulled me toward building software. I'm now learning to design and ship real apps and websites by vibe coding: describing what I want, collaborating with AI to build it, and applying the same analytical rigor I bring to research.

This site is where that journey lives — the courses I'm working through, the projects taking shape, and (soon) the things I ship.
